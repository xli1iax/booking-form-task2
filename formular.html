We are GameZone team


Hello! We are the GameZone team that will make your evening unforgettable! If you love games, this is the place for you. We have everything from board games to escape rooms, and that's not all. We have been giving memories for twenty years!


In addition to classic entertainment, we are constantly expanding and adding new ones. We have recently opened a computer gaming area where you can immerse yourself in the fascinating world of video games. Modern computers, the latest releases, and comfortable gaming spaces - all this awaits you in GameZone.


In addition, we are pleased to announce the opening of a billiard room and a bowling club. These are ideal places for a fun time with friends or corporate events. Whether it's a bowling competition or a game of billiards, active recreation is guaranteed!


Our escape room continues to surprise with new plots and challenges. Can you solve all the puzzles and get out on time?


Come to us and spend your time unforgettably. Everyone will find something for themselves in GameZone. An evening with us is an evening filled with laughter, excitement and pleasant memories.
